About Me

Hello! I'm Nadia, a professional hairdresser with over 30 years of experience. Since 1994, I've dedicated my work to hairstyling, hair care and the well-being of my clients.

After many years in salons, I chose to bring my services directly to your home, offering professionalism, high-quality products and personalised care in a comfortable, familiar setting.

I specialise in services for:

  • elderly clients
  • people with reduced mobility
  • people living in nursing homes or care homes
  • anyone who prefers the comfort of home
