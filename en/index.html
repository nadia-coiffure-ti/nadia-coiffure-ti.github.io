About Me

Hello! I'm Nadia, a professional hairdresser since 1994. I've dedicated my life to the art of hairstyling, with a special passion for hair care and the wellbeing of my clients.

After years of experience in salons, I've chosen to offer my services directly at your home – professionalism, quality products, and personalized treatment in the comfort of your home.

Specialized in services for:

  • Elderly people
  • People with reduced mobility
  • Residents in nursing homes
  • Those who prefer home comfort
